Find Your Path in the History MA at VU Amsterdam

At VU Amsterdam, we believe history is more than understanding the past—it's essential for navigating the present. We train critically engaged historians who bring historical perspective to today's urgent challenges: inequality, climate change, colonial legacies, and democratic fragility.

Why study the History MA at VU Amsterdam?

Our MA programme combines rigorous scholarship with real-world impact. You'll work closely with internationally recognized faculty in small seminars, gain professional experience through integrated internships (the only Dutch history MA to do so), and build expertise in Amsterdam—a city where global histories intersect.

We don't believe in one-size-fits-all education. That's why we offer four specialized profiles, each combining core historical training with distinctive interdisciplinary approaches. Whether you're passionate about social movements, colonial justice, religious cultures, or environmental crises, you'll find your path here.
